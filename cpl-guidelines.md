# CPL (Credit for Prior Learning) Guidelines
## CCC Google Data Analytics Certificate Pathway

---

## What Is CPL?

Credit for Prior Learning (CPL) allows students to earn college credit for knowledge
and skills they have already acquired through work experience, industry certifications,
military training, or self-directed learning. Instead of completing the full course,
students demonstrate their competency through skills verification assessments.

CPL is supported by California Education Code Section 79500 and the California
Community Colleges Chancellor's Office CPL guidelines.

## Skills Verification Requirements

### Tier 1 Courses (DA-101 through DA-105)

Students must complete the same skills verification assignments used in the regular
course. These are hands-on assignments that demonstrate practical competency, NOT
multiple-choice tests. Each assignment requires original work.

### Tier 2 Courses (DA-201 through DA-205)

Students must complete a condensed assessment that covers the key competencies of
the full course. This may include:

- Written analysis demonstrating domain knowledge
- Technical assignments (SQL queries, Python code, Tableau dashboards)
- A portfolio artifact demonstrating applied skills
- An oral or written reflection connecting skills to professional practice

## How Students Demonstrate Prior Learning

### Eligible Prior Learning Sources

- **Industry certifications:** Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate,
  IBM Data Analyst Professional Certificate, or equivalent
- **Work experience:** Current or recent employment as a data analyst, business
  analyst, or similar role (minimum 1 year)
- **Military training:** Data analysis, intelligence analysis, or IT-related MOS/rating
- **Self-directed learning:** Documented completion of equivalent coursework or
  training programs

### Documentation Required

Students must submit:

1. **CPL petition form** (provided by the institution)
2. **Evidence of prior learning** (certificate, resume, work samples, or employer letter)
3. **Completed skills verification assessments** from the CPL course package

---

## CPL Course Structure

Each CPL course IMSCC package contains only two modules:

1. **Course Orientation**
   - Welcome page explaining the CPL process
   - Syllabus with assessment requirements

2. **Skills Verification**
   - The same skills verification assignments from the regular course (Tier 1)
   - A condensed assessment portfolio (Tier 2)

Students do NOT complete Coursera courses, weekly modules, quizzes, or discussions
in the CPL version. The focus is entirely on demonstrating existing competency.

---

## Grading

CPL courses use a single assignment group:

- **Skills Verification: 100%**

Students must earn a passing grade (C or better, 70%) on the skills verification
assessments to receive credit.

## Sample CPL Petition Language

Institutions may adapt the following language for their CPL petition forms:

> **Petition for Credit for Prior Learning**
>
> Course: [DA-XXX: Course Title]
>
> I am requesting Credit for Prior Learning for the above course. I have acquired
> the knowledge and skills covered in this course through:
>
> [ ] Industry certification: _______________
> [ ] Work experience: _______________ (employer, role, dates)
> [ ] Military training: _______________ (MOS/rating, dates)
> [ ] Self-directed learning: _______________ (program, dates)
>
> I understand that I must complete the skills verification assessments in the
> CPL course package and earn a passing grade to receive credit. I agree to
> submit all required documentation.
>
> Student Signature: _______________ Date: _______________
> Student ID: _______________

## Institutional Implementation

### For Counselors/Advisors

- Review student's prior learning documentation before approving CPL enrollment
- Verify that the student's experience aligns with the course competencies
- Import the CPL IMSCC package into Canvas (separate from the regular course)
- Set a reasonable deadline for assessment completion (typically 2-4 weeks)

### For Instructors

- Review submitted assessments using the same rubrics as the regular course
- Provide feedback even though this is a CPL course — students benefit from it
- Report grades through the normal institutional process
- Contact the student if assessments do not demonstrate sufficient competency

### For Administrators

- CPL courses generate FTES the same as regular courses
- Ensure your institution's CPL policy covers these courses
- The CPL IMSCC packages are separate from the regular course packages
- Track CPL enrollment and success rates for program review
