Credit for Prior Learning (CPL)

Students who have already completed Google Career Certificates elsewhere — through Coursera, employer programs, or self-study — can earn CCC credit quickly through CPL assessment. No need to retake courses. This accelerates time to degree and validates real-world skills.

First Rollout

Data Analytics Certificate

The Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate is the first to receive full CCC course development, informed by statewide faculty expertise.

In January 2026, a faculty focus group of data analytics instructors from across California convened to evaluate, package, and align Coursera content to community college standards. Their recommendations shaped 19 Canvas-ready courses spanning two tiers of instruction.

The focus group achieved strong consensus on key structural decisions that will guide future certificate rollouts across all nine Google career pathways.

Focus Group Outcomes

  • 75% consensus on a 12-unit certificate structure
  • 88% preference for hybrid packaging of Coursera content
  • 100% agreement on industry-relevant assessments

Strategic Alignment

Vision 2030 Alignment

This initiative directly supports the California Community Colleges' Vision 2030 goals.

Student Success & Equity

No-cost access to industry certificates removes financial barriers and ensures all students — regardless of background — can earn credentials valued by employers. CPL pathways honor prior learning and accelerate completion.

Workforce Alignment

Google Career Certificates target high-demand fields — data analytics, cybersecurity, IT support, UX design, project management, and more. Graduates enter the workforce with skills employers actively seek.

Institutional Capacity

Ready-made Canvas course shells, Creative Commons licensing, and systemwide coordination reduce the burden on individual colleges. Faculty focus on teaching, not course development from scratch.
