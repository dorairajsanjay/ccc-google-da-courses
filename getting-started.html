Getting Started

Everything your college needs to adopt Google Career Certificates and integrate them into your programs.

Step by Step

Recommended Steps for Colleges

A clear path from interest to implementation.

1

Designate a Program Lead

Identify a faculty or administrative lead to coordinate certificate integration at your college. This person will serve as the primary contact with the Chancellor's Office and the faculty community of practice.

2

Access Coursera Scholarships

Complete Google's institutional access form to receive free Coursera scholarships. Create a Coursera for Campus account using the same email address. Add additional administrators as needed. No cost is involved.

3

Choose an Integration Model

Review the five packaging options identified by the faculty focus group and select the approach that fits your college's existing programs, curriculum approval timelines, and student needs:

  • Full Certificate (12 units) — standalone credential
  • Embedded Modules — integrate into existing courses
  • Stackable Micro-credentials — flexible for working adults
  • Non-Credit / CE — fastest to implement
  • Hybrid Credit + Google Certificate — dual credential
4

Import Canvas Course Shells

All 19 Data Analytics courses are available on Canvas Commons under CC BY 4.0. To import:

  • Go to Settings → Import Course Content
  • Select Canvas Course Export Package
  • Upload the .imscc file and click Import
  • Review the Instructor Resources module (hidden from students)

Browse all 19 courses →

5

Customize for Your College

After importing, replace placeholders with your institution's information:

  • [Your College] → your institution name
  • [Instructor Name] → your name
  • [Semester] → current term
  • [instructor-email@college.edu] → your email

Set due dates and publish modules as you go.

6

Coordinate with IT

If planning Canvas LTI integration with Coursera, engage IT early to test connectivity and address login/authentication in your environment. 88% of focus group faculty anticipated login challenges at colleges using Microsoft environments.

7

Explore CPL Pathways

For students with existing Google certificates, import the CPL course variants — pre-built with only orientation and skills assessments. Contact Samuel Lee at CCCCO (slee@cccco.edu) for systemwide CPL guidance.

Learn about CPL →

8

Join the Community of Practice

Connect with faculty across the state who are integrating Google certificates. The community provides shared resources, templates, peer support, and professional development credit.

  • Async channels (Slack/Discord)
  • Quarterly meetings
  • Shared resources and templates
